Nature
Rute, is located in the Sierras Subbéticas Natural Park, forming part of the mountainous nucleus called Sierra de Rute y Horconera. In its surroundings there are places of great scenic beauty that are a good attraction for hiking and other activities such as mountain biking, paragliding, caving, climbing, motocross, horseback riding, as well as countless natural viewpoints from where you can enjoy views spectacular.
From the faunal point of view, there is a great variety of interesting species, such as populations of birds of prey: eagle owl, kestrel, Bonelli's eagle, buzzard, goshawk, hawk, little owl, owls, highlighting the peregrine falcon, symbol of the Sierras Subbéticas Natural Park and the Griffon Vulture. Among the mammals, we can highlight the wild boar, badgers, hares and rabbits, ferrets, genets and foxes, as well as some mountain goats.
